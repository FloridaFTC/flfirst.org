**2. How to Watch**

Everyone in the Florida _FIRST_ Tech Challenge community is invited to attend the Florida Championship on March 6–7. We’ll begin qualification matches after 4pm ET on Friday afternoon, and continue the festivities starting at Saturday morning around 8am ET.

If you can’t join us in person, you can watch our live streams for each division. They’ll broadcast the same stream during ceremonies, awards, and finals; and show each individual division during qualification matches. Find all four streams (Lawrence & Scott Division, Day 1 & 2) here: https://www.youtube.com/playlist?list=PLvY2mfgD-92H_eqvZ1PnIFFsHUhYwZbRb.
